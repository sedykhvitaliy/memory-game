Game Rules

  • 1.Click on a card to flip it.
  • 2.Flip two cards to find a match.
  • 3.If the cards match, they remain flipped.
  • 4.If they don't match, they flip back.
  • 5.The game starts when you flip the first card.
  • 6.You win when all cards are matched.
  • 7.The game ends when the timer runs out.
  • 8.Click "Stop" to reset the game.
